1. Roll 3d6 for Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. If none of your ability scores are 14 or higher, you may roll a new set of six scores.
2. Choose an [[RULES/1 - Ancestries/Tome of Ancestry\|Ancestry]] and note its benefits.
3. Choose a [[RULES/1 - Classes/Tome of Classes\|Class]] and note its benefits. HP is your Class Hit Dice + CON,  Roll once on the Talent table.
4. Choose two [[RULES/1 - Backgrounds/Tome of Backgrounds\|Backgrounds]]. These represent your character's knowledge and experience before adventuring.
5. Choose an [[RULES/Alignment\|Alignment]]. This serves as a guideline for your character’s general behavior and values
6. Roll 2d6 × 5 to determine your starting gold. Use this to purchase [[RULES/2 - Adventuring Gear/Adventuring Gear\|Gear]], [[RULES/2 - Armor/Armor\|Armor]], and [[RULES/2 - Weapons/Weapons\|Weapons]].
7. Name your character.
