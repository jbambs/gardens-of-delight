- **Lawful**: Follows rules, traditions, or orders.
- **Chaotic**: Values freedom and personal choice over rules.
- **Neutral**: Neither strongly follows nor rejects either side.

- **Good**: Tries to help others and avoid harm.
- **Evil**: Puts personal gain or harm to others first.


Alignment can change over time if a character consistently acts differently.
